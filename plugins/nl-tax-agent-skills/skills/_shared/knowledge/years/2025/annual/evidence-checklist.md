## Rule

Before preparing a workpack for the 2025 annual return (aangifte inkomstenbelasting), confirm that the taxpayer has gathered all relevant documents. This checklist is used during evidence collection to ensure completeness. Items marked as required are mandatory for filing; items marked as conditional apply only when the taxpayer's situation includes that category.

These are reference notes for workpack preparation -- not final tax advice.

## Personal details (persoonsgegevens) -- required

- BSN (burgerservicenummer): do NOT collect the BSN itself; only confirm the taxpayer has it available for the official portal. The BSN is entered directly into Mijn Belastingdienst, never stored in a workpack.
- Date of birth (geboortedatum): needed to determine AOW-age status, which affects tax rates and credits.
- Fiscal partner status (fiscaal partnerschap): confirm whether the taxpayer had a fiscal partner on 31 December 2025 or qualifies as fiscal partner for part of the year. This affects allocation of income, deductions, and box 3 assets.
- Address and residence history: confirm Dutch tax residency for the full year 2025, or note partial residency dates.

## Income from employment and benefits (inkomen uit werk en uitkeringen) -- required

- Jaaropgaaf (annual salary statement) from each employer: shows gross salary, wage tax withheld (loonheffing), and social contributions. Collect one per employer if the taxpayer had multiple jobs.
- Pension statements (pensioenuitkering jaaroverzicht): annual summary from pension provider showing gross pension income and tax withheld.
- Benefit statements from UWV: for WW (unemployment), WIA/WAO (disability), ZW (sickness). The UWV jaaropgaaf shows amounts and withholdings.
- Benefit statements from SVB: for AOW (state pension), Anw (survivor pension), or AKW (child benefit). The SVB jaaropgaaf is needed.
- Freelance / self-employment income records (winst uit onderneming): profit and loss statement, balance sheet, hours administration for zelfstandigenaftrek (requires 1,225+ hours). Relevant for IB-ondernemers.
- Income from other activities (resultaat uit overige werkzaamheden): e.g., freelance work not qualifying as onderneming, board member fees.

## Own home (eigen woning) -- conditional

- WOZ-beschikking (WOZ value notice): the official valuation of the property for tax year 2025. The WOZ-waarde as determined by the municipality is used for eigenwoningforfait calculation.
- Mortgage annual statement (jaaroverzicht hypotheek): from the mortgage lender, showing total interest paid, principal repaid, and outstanding balance as of 31 December 2025.
- Mortgage interest paid (hypotheekrente betaald): total deductible interest for the year. Verify the mortgage qualifies for deduction (annuitair or lineair for post-2013 mortgages).
- Eigenwoningforfait: calculated as a percentage of WOZ-waarde. See own-home.md for the applicable percentages.
- Details of any second home or rental property (these go to box 3, not box 1).

## Savings and investments (sparen en beleggen / box 3) -- conditional

- Bank statements showing balances on 1 January 2025 and 31 December 2025: needed for box 3 calculation. Collect for all bank accounts (savings, current, deposit).
- Investment portfolio year-end statements (eindejaarsoverzicht beleggingen): showing market value of stocks, bonds, mutual funds as of 1 January and 31 December 2025.
- Crypto holdings: market value of cryptocurrency holdings as of 1 January and 31 December 2025. The Belastingdienst treats crypto as overige bezittingen in box 3.
- Other assets: second homes, loans to others (vorderingen), participations not qualifying for box 2.
- Debts (schulden): outstanding debts as of 1 January and 31 December 2025 that qualify as box 3 debts. Note the threshold (drempel) below which debts are not deductible.

## Deductions (aftrekposten) -- conditional

- Healthcare costs (specifieke zorgkosten): receipts and summaries for medical expenses not reimbursed by insurance, including prescribed medications, medical aids, travel costs to medical facilities, dietary costs on medical prescription.
- Gifts (giften): receipts or bank statements for donations to ANBI-registered institutions (Algemeen Nut Beogende Instelling). Distinguish between periodic gifts (periodieke giften via notarial deed or written agreement) and incidental gifts (with threshold).
- Study costs (studiekosten / scholingsuitgaven): largely replaced by STAP-budget, but residual deductibility may apply. Collect receipts for tuition and mandatory study materials.
- Alimentatie (maintenance payments): documentation of alimony paid to ex-partner (partneralimentatie is deductible; kinderalimentatie is not). Court order or notarial deed showing the obligation.
- Alimentatie received: jaaropgaaf of alimentatie received (this is taxable income in box 1).
- Lijfrentepremie (annuity premium): annual statement from the insurer or bank showing premiums paid. Needed for jaarruimte/reserveringsruimte calculation.

## Other situations -- conditional

- Foreign income declarations: income earned outside the Netherlands. Needed to determine whether double-taxation relief (voorkoming dubbele belasting) applies. Collect foreign tax statements.
- Capital insurance policies (kapitaalverzekeringen): policies taken before 2001 may have transitional rules (overgangsrecht). Collect policy details and current value.
- Box 2 income: dividends or capital gains from substantial holdings (aanmerkelijk belang, ownership >= 5% in a BV). Collect dividend statements and transaction records.
- Green investments (groene beleggingen): qualifying green investment certificates for potential vrijstelling in box 3.

## Notes

- The VIA (vooringevulde aangifte) will pre-fill many of these items, but the taxpayer must verify all pre-filled data against source documents. VIA data can be incomplete or delayed.
- For fiscal partners, collect documents for both partners as they file a joint return with allocation choices.
- Do not collect DigiD credentials. See security/digid.md.
