# Manual Steps -- Stopping a Voorlopige Aanslag (Stopzetten)

## Steps

1. Go to Mijn Belastingdienst (mijn.belastingdienst.nl)
2. Log in with DigiD
3. Navigate to voorlopige aanslag 2026
4. Select the option to stop (stopzetten)
5. Confirm that you want to stop monthly payments/refunds
6. Monthly payments/refunds will stop
7. Final settlement happens at annual return time

## Note

If you currently pay monthly and want a different amount rather than stopping entirely, use the CHANGE (wijzigen) process instead of stopzetten. Changing allows you to adjust the monthly amount up or down; stopping ends all monthly payments/refunds until the final annual settlement.
