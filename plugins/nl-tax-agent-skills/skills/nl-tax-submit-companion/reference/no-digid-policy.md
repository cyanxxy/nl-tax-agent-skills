# DigiD Policy -- Submission Companion

## Core policy

- This skill does **NOT** collect, store, or use DigiD credentials.
- DigiD is required for **ALL** submission paths (annual return and voorlopige aanslag).
- The workpack and field map are designed for **MANUAL** entry in the official Belastingdienst portal.
- **Never** paste DigiD credentials into any file, chat, or tool.

## If you don't have DigiD

You need to apply at your municipality (gemeente). The application process takes several days as the activation code is sent by post. Plan ahead if you have an upcoming filing deadline.

## If someone helps you

They need DigiD Machtigen authorization. This is a formal process where you grant another person permission to act on your behalf through the Belastingdienst portal. The helper uses their own DigiD to log in after being authorized.

## Paper filing

Paper filing may be possible in exceptional cases. Contact the BelastingTelefoon at **0800-0543** (free within the Netherlands) to discuss your situation. This is not the standard path and may not be available for all return types.

## What this skill provides

This skill prepares a submission checklist and guides you through the manual steps. All actual interactions with the Belastingdienst portal -- logging in, entering data, reviewing, signing, and submitting -- must be performed by you (or your authorized representative) directly.
